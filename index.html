Our Story

A Church Built on
Faith, Family & Fellowship

Grace Covenant Church was founded in 2000 on a simple conviction: that the local church is the hope of the world. For over two decades, we have gathered as a diverse community — hungry for God, passionate about people, and committed to transformation.

Whether you are exploring faith for the first time or have walked with God for decades, there is a place for you here. We believe every person carries a God-given purpose, and we exist to help you discover and live it.
